KNOW-N-GROW, a device that measures the moisture levels in soil that lets the farmer know if he/she needs to further irrigate the crops.
Further, a device will be integrated that lets the farmer know when to stop watering the crops by measuring the water output and the weather conditions.

HOW THE SENSOR WORK
  • The Soil Moisture Sensor uses capacitance to measure the dielectric permittivity of the surrounding medium.
  • In soil, dielectric permittivity is a function of the water content.
  • The sensor creates a voltage proportional to the dielectric permittivity, and therefore the water content of the soil.
